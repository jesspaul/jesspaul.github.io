About Me

My name is Jessica and I am a self-taught developer who enjoys working in HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. I aspire to be a front-end developer working within a great team as part of a small company. Currently I work as an administration coordinator for a large automotive manufacturing company. I learned to code because I want to make a positive impact on my community through meaningful work.

I live in Athens, GA with my boyfriend and two cats. In my free time I love to crochet amigurumi - the Japanese art of creating stuffed yarn creatures. I also enjoy baking, sculpting with polymer clay, and playing video games. I also love to travel and would like to make it a bigger priority in my life once it is safe to do so again.
