About Me

My name is Jessica and I am a self-taught developer who enjoys working in HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. I aspire to be a front-end developer working within a great team as part of a small company. Currently I work as an office administrator in a large manufacturing company. I am driven by my desire to do meaningful work that makes a positive impact on my community. I believe that working within the Technology industry is the perfect opportunity to couple my passion to make a difference with my technical skills.

I live in Athens, GA with my boyfriend and two cats. In my free time I love to crochet amigurumi - the Japanese art of creating stuffed yarn creatures. I also enjoy baking cupcakes and sculpting with polymer clay. Having been to a few countries in Europe, I would like to make travel a bigger priority in my life. Experiencing new cultures, foods, and scenery has influenced me to think beyond my worldview.
